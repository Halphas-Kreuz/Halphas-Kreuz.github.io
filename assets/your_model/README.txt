Put your Cubism2 model here.

Expected:
  /assets/your_model/model.json
  /assets/your_model/model.moc
  /assets/your_model/model.512/ (or model.1024/, etc)
  /assets/your_model/textures/ (optional)
  /assets/your_model/motions/ (optional)

Then the site will load it via /assets/live2d-c2.js (MODEL_JSON).
